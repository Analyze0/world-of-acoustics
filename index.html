What is sound?

    Sound is a vibration in the form of a wave. This wave interacts with materials through and off of materials. The materials it passes through affects how the waves change. These waves become sound when they enter your ears and your brain translates them into something you can understand. These waves have different frequencies. The human brain can only pick up sounds with certain frequencies. Age is also a factor in your brain interpreting sound waves.
