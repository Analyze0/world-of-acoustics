The properties of sound.

What are the properties of sound?

  • Wavelength

    • Wave Length is the distance between crests of a wave.

  • Amplitude

    • Amplitude is the height from the bottom of the wave to the crest.

  • Time-Period

    • Time period is the time it takes between two crests in a wave.

  • Frequency

    • Frequency is the pitch of the sound of a wave and is the number of waves that pass by in a second.

  • Velocity or Speed

    • It is the frequency of the wave times the frequency of the wave.
